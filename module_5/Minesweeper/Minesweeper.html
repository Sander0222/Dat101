Information on debuger mode: 
The right click is disabled in the game, becouse of the insert of flags.
On Chrome and Firefox, Press F12 (rember if you have a "FN" key, you might need to press "FN" + F12) to open the developer console.
On safari in macOS, you need to enable the developer menu in the preferences and press Option+Command+I (⌥+⌘+I).
